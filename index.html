Plastic surgeon

Rhinoplasty is very simple and precise. The specialist surgeon changes the structure and general shape of the nose, whether by removing or adding part of the bone or cartilage. The result is a reduction or enlargement of the nose to obtain the ideal shape or the shape agreed upon between the doctor and the patient.
